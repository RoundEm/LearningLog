Learning Log

Learning a new subject can be hard, but by maintaining a Learning Log you can easily record and reference journal-like entries that will help you retain the right information, track your progress and help ensure that you're staying productive and motivated.

Retain the important information and filter out the bad.

Keep track of new ideas and good resources that you'd like to come back to later.

Track your progress for later reflection and to ensure that you're staying on track.

Track your notable achievements or sage wisdom youp've encountered to help keep you motivated.

Record aha moments or anything that helps lead you to achieve a deeper understanding, such as specific modes of thought, analogies or examples.

Add tags to each entry so that you can easily sort and find information later.
