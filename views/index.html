Learning Log

Learning a new subject can be hard, but by maintaining a learning log you can easily record and reference journal-like entries that will help you retain the right information, track your progress and maintain productivity.

Benefits of using a learning log:

  • Retain the important information and filter out the bad
  • Track your progress for later reflection and to ensure that you're staying on track
  • Keep track of new ideas and good resources that you'd like to come back to later
  • Track your notable achievements and sage wisdom you encounter to help keep you motivated
  • Record aha moments or anything that helps you achieve a deeper understanding, such as specific modes of thought, analogies or examples
  • Add tags to each entry so that you can easily sort and find information later
